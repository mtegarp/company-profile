Introduction

Established in 2023, PT Maju Bangun Sinergi (MBS) is a dynamic and forward-thinking company operating in the fields of engineering, interior/exterior design, and digital technology solutions. With a strong focus on civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering, as well as expertise in cutting-edge automation and IT-related services, MBS offers comprehensive solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Industry Sectors:

  1. Engineering: Our highly skilled engineering team specializes in civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering. We provide end-to-end engineering solutions, including conceptualization, detailed design, project management, and construction supervision. Whether it's infrastructure development, industrial optimization, or specialized engineering projects, our team's prowess ensures the highest quality standards and optimal performance.
  2. Interior/Exterior Design: MBS understands the importance of creating functional and visually appealing spaces.
  3. Digital Technology: With the rapid advancement of digital technology, MBS stays at the forefront by offering cutting-edge automation and IT-related services.
